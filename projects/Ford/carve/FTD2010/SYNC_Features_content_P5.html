Emergency Assist

This isn’t just any 4WD. Every 16 milliseconds the Kuga adjusts which wheels get torque, so your cornering and traction is always at an optimum. Rain, hail, snow or shine.

In case of Emergency (ICE) Contacts

Just drive it. Preferably on a road with the most twists and turns you can find. It’s the only way to truly appreciate how well the Kuga handles. In corners, torque is automatically transferred to the wheels that have the most grip, so the front end hugs the inside of every bend. Under-steering is virtually eliminated.
