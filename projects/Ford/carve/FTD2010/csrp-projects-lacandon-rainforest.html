Let's Save the Lacandon Rainforest

Although it is one of the places with most biodiversity, it has suffered from great damage. In the last 20 years, more than 41% of its area has been destroyed. In order to protect this place, which provides 30% of the country's sweet water, from 2001 to 2007 Ford and its Dealers' Civic Committee started a program called "Let's Save the Lacandonan Rainforest".
