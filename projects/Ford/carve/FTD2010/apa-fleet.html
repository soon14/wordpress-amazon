The Fleet Business Center

About Ford Fleet

The Fleet Business Centre is a business unit dedicated to providing a high level of customised service to all our clients, whether your business is Corporate, Government or Rental. Ford’s position as a world leader in the automotive industry allows us to leverage our extensive experience, technology, and resources to exceeed all your fleet expectations.

Smart Vehicle Choices

Ford has one of the strongest product offerings available, with a vehicle to suit every type of business - regardless of industry. No other automaker can deliver so completely on the criteria most important to your company.

  • SAFETY: Ford has one of the largest selections of vehicles with a 5-Star ANCAP safety rating
  • SMART: The calling card of Ford vehicles is smart technology,ranging from Bluetooth® connectivity to exceptional off-road and towing power
  • SUSTAINABLE: Our award-winning line of EcoBoost engines deliver reduced emissions without sacrificing a bit of performance
  • QUALITY: We’re committed to helping you get the most out of your Ford fleet, with a reduced cost of ownership and comprehensive servicing

Meeting your needs locally

Getting to know your fleet requirements is critical to making sure we provide the best solutions to fit your business needs.

Our Ford Business Centre’s Dealer Network has numerous locations around the country, with extensive coverage of city and country areas.

This means we can quickly and efficiently handle all your business inquiries, while ensuring that vehicle transactions and deliveries are as smooth and hassle-free as possible.

Meeting your needs globally

The Ford Business Centre works closely with Ford Motor Company’s Global Fleet Solutions, allowing us to provide you with fleet arrangements that are both extensive and personalised.

Global Fleet Solutions is an international team comprised of representatives from every Ford Motor Company worldwide. The team is uniquely specialised to manage complex fleet requirements from multinational clients spanning multiple countries.

Ford’s uniquely extensive and diversified global product portfolio not only delivers vehicles to match any fleet requirement, but gives you room to pick and choose.
