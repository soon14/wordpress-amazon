WARRANTIES

Warranty Overview

Buying a new or pre-owned Ford vehicle is a smart choice, and to ensure the experience continues it is important to select the right myFord warranty for your vehicle. To review the myFord Warranty range in detail, please select the individual product links below.

Extended New Vehicle Warranty

The myFord Extended New Vehicle Warranty is a full extension of the original Ford Express New Vehicle Warranty, with plan options available to extend the original three years/100,000 km, by a further three years/100,000 km. This means your Ford can have new vehicle warranty protection for up to six years from first registration or up to 200,000km (since new), whichever occurs first.

The myFord Extended New Vehicle Warranty is available at the time of your Ford purchase from a participating myFord Authorised Dealer and for existing owners. Vehicles that are under three years of age from the date of first registration and have travelled less than 100,000kms at the time of application are eligible to apply.

Please click here to download the myFord Extended New Vehicle Warranty Product Disclosure Statement and Warranty Booklet for full details on coverage and exclusions.

Factory Approved Warranty

The myFord Factory Approved Warranty has been designed to provide extensive warranty coverage for a wide range of mechanical components. Covering over 4,500 individual components, this warranty offers an affordable way to secure motoring peace of mind. The myFord Factory Approved Warranty provides plan options to protect your Ford for up to a further four years and up to a total of 200,000km (since new).

myFord Factory Approved Warranty is available at the time of your Ford purchase from a participating myFord Authorised Dealer and for existing owners. Vehicles that are under three years of age from the date of first registration and have travelled less than 100,000kms at the time of application are eligible to apply.

Please click here to download the myFord Factory Approved Product Disclosure Statement and Warranty Booklet for full details on coverage and exclusions.

Vehicle Warranty

Vehicle Warranty - Essentials

Overview
Available on both new and pre-owned vehicles, myFord Vehicle Warranty – Essentials offers entry level warranty coverage within the myFord Vehicle Warranty range. The warranty includes major mechanical component coverage to over 500 parts and provides plan options to protect your vehicle for up to a further four years and unlimited kms.

Eligibility
myFord Vehicle Warranty – Essentials is only available at the time of vehicle purchase from a participating myFord Authorised Dealer. It is available for a wide range of vehicles up to 20 years of age from first registration and have travelled less than 260,000kms at the time of application.

Please click here to download the myFord Vehicle Warranty Product Disclosure Statement and Warranty Booklet for full details on coverage and exclusions.

Vehicle Warranty - Essentials+

Overview
myFord Vehicle Warranty - Essentials+ offers mid level warranty coverage within the myFord Vehicle Warranty range, for both new and pre-owned vehicles. The warranty includes major mechanical component coverage to over 1,000 parts and plan options to protect your vehicle for up to a further four years with unlimited kms.

Eligibility
myFord Vehicle Warranty - Essentials+ is only available at the time of purchase from a participating myFord Authorised Dealer. It is available for a wide range of vehicles up to 10 years of age from first registration and have travelled less than 160,000kms at the time of application.

Please click here to download the myFord Vehicle Warranty Product Disclosure Statement and Warranty Booklet for full details on coverage and exclusions.

Vehicle Warranty - Advantage

Overview
myFord Vehicle Warranty - Advantage offers high level warranty coverage within the myFord Vehicle Warranty range with extensive protection for both new and pre-owned vehicles. The warranty provides plan options to protect your vehicle for up to a further four years and up to a total of 200,000kms (since new).

Eligibility
myFord Vehicle Warranty - Advantage is only available at the time of vehicle purchase from a participating myFord Authorised Dealer. It is available for a wide range of vehicles up to 10 years from first registration and that have travelled less than 150,000kms at the time of application.

Please click here to download the myFord Vehicle Warranty Product Disclosure Statement and Warranty Booklet for full details on coverage and exclusions.
